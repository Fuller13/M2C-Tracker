Sample Informational Interview Questions

Here are some questions you might consider asking during an interview:

  • What does the day to day in your role look like?
  • What skills are important for your role?
  • Are there any Veteran organizations in your company?
  • What else should I be doing to give myself a higher market value?
  • How did you get where you are today?
  • What is the biggest challenge you face in your role?
  • What's something you wish you knew before starting?
  • What's the best professional advice you've received?
  • How is the work life balance in your company?
  • What are the biggest challenges you see in the future for your company?
