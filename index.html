About Military Transition Tracker

Military Transition Tracker is a comprehensive tool designed to support military personnel in their transition to civilian life, offering a centralized platform to manage to-dos, timelines, contacts, and more. Built by a transitioning U.S. Navy veteran, this project aims to simplify the transition process and empower veterans to stay organized and prepared. With features like data persistence and Excel export, Transition Tracker ensures you can access and share your transition plans anytime, anywhere.

This tool is designed for offline use. Data entered on this website will not be saved or accessible later. Please choose one of the following download options below to start taking control of your transition!

Prefer to use the site as seen? Download the site

Once you have downloaded the website file:

1. Extract all files from the downloaded zip file.

2. Open the the file called "Index" in your preferred browser.

3. Begin taking control of your transition!

Note: Ensure you always use the same browser.
