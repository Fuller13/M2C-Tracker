Welcome to Military Transition Tracker

A comprehensive tool for military-to-civilian (M2C) transition, built by Veterans for Veterans

Military Transition Tracker is a comprehensive tool designed to support military personnel in their transition to civilian life, offering a centralized platform to manage to-dos, timelines, contacts, and more. Built by veterans, this tool aims to simplify the transition process and empower veterans to stay organized and prepared. With features like data persistence and JSON export, Transition Tracker ensures you can access and share your transition plans anytime, anywhere.

About Military Transition Tracker

This tool is designed for offline use, allowing you to manage your transition data locally on your computer. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Download the Website: Click the link below to download the ZIP file containing the website files.
  2. Extract the Files: Unzip the downloaded file to a folder on your computer.
  3. Open the Website: Locate the index.html file in the extracted folder and double-click to open it in your preferred web browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Edge).
  4. Add and Edit Data: Navigate to pages like To-Dos, Contacts, or Timeline to add new entries, edit table cells, or delete rows. Your changes are automatically saved in your browser.
  5. Save Your Data: Click the "Save Changes" button on any page to ensure your data is stored. Data persists in your browser as long as you use the same browser and don’t clear its storage.
  6. Download Your Data: Click the "Download Data" button on any page to save all your data as a m2c-data.json file for backup or sharing.
  7. Access Your Data: Reopen index.html in the same browser to view or edit your saved data.
