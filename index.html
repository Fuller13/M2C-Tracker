About Transition Tracker

Transition Tracker is a comprehensive tool designed to support military personnel in their transition to civilian life, offering a centralized platform to manage to-dos, timelines, contacts, and more. Built by a transitioning U.S. Navy veteran, this project aims to simplify the transition process and empower veterans to stay organized and prepared. With features like data persistence and Excel export, Transition Tracker ensures you can access and share your transition plans anytime, anywhere.
